> **BLUF**: 李飞飞最新著作《Agent AI》中文版发布，涵盖多模态交互、强化学习等前沿领域，为AI学者和工程师提供了深度解析。

## 为什么值得关注？

李飞飞教授最新出版的《Agent AI: 多模态交互视野的考察》中文版正式发布，这是AI领域的一次重磅更新。这份由117页组成的深度文献，不仅详细解析了Agent AI的基本概念，还深入探讨了多模态交互、强化学习（RL）与人类-计算机交互（HCI）的未来方向。

在当前AI技术快速演进的背景下，这本书提供了从理论到实践的完整视角，尤其对关注AI、HCI和机器人技术的学者和工程师而言，是一份不可错过的资料。

## 它解决了什么问题？

《Agent AI》一书聚焦于AI Agent的发展，特别是LLM（大型语言模型）和VLM（视觉语言模型）在具身AI（Embodied AI）中的应用。它提出了许多关键问题，例如：如何将语言和视觉能力整合到同一AI系统中？如何在真实世界环境中使AI Agent具备自我学习和决策能力？

书中还特别讨论了强化学习（RL）如何借助LLM设计奖励函数，使AI在复杂任务中更高效地学习和适应。此外，书中还回顾了HCI（人机交互）的最新进展，并指出了未来的研究方向。

## 为什么是中文版？

在AI领域，英文文献占据主导地位，但《Agent AI》中文版的推出，让中文读者能够更便捷地掌握这些前沿技术。书中内容经过专业翻译，不仅保留了原文的科学严谨性，还结合了中文读者的阅读习惯，提高了可读性。

## Why is This Worth Paying Attention To?

Li Fei-Fei’s latest publication, *"Agent AI: A Multimodal Perspective"*, has now been released in Chinese. This 117-page academic work provides a comprehensive overview of Agent AI, focusing on the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs) in embodied AI. It’s a timely and essential read for those in the AI, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and robotics fields.

## What Does It Solve?

The book addresses key challenges in AI development, such as how to combine language and visual intelligence into a unified AI system, and how to enable AI agents to learn and make decisions in real-world environments. It also explores how Large Language Models can be used to design more efficient reinforcement learning (RL) systems, and how this can be applied to complex tasks.

## Why the Chinese Edition?

While English-language AI research dominates the global discourse, the release of the Chinese edition of *"Agent AI"* allows Chinese researchers and practitioners to engage more deeply with these cutting-edge topics. The translation maintains the scientific rigor of the original while being tailored for Chinese readers, making it both accessible and valuable.
